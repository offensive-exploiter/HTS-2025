Overview

HTS 2025 is designed to give participants practical experience across forensics, reversing, and project development. Everyone is welcome! No prior experience required.

Scoring

  • Challenge rounds (flags/reversing): 50 pts
  • Project development: 30 pts
  • LinkedIn activity: 10 pts
  • Report/documentation: 10 pts

Rules

  1. Everyone is welcomed and respected.
  2. Teams must consist of exactly 2 members.
  3. Submissions must be original work.
  4. Daily LinkedIn posts required for the LinkedIn score.
  5. Reports must follow the official format.
  6. Organizers’ decisions are final.

Rounds

Round 1 — Folder Forensics / Cryptography Beginner

Find 9 flags across a large dataset. Tools: strings, steghide, binwalk, exiftool. Time: 60–90 minutes.

Round 2 — Reverse & Recover Intermediate

Reverse safe Windows executables to extract flags. Tools allowed: Ghidra, IDA Free, x64dbg, radare2. Time: 2–3 hours.

Round 3 — Build a Project Advanced

Build a cybersecurity-related mini-project. Demo + README + short report. Judging focuses on functionality, originality, code quality, documentation, and presentation.

Prizes & Recognition

Top teams receive official certificates and online exposure.
